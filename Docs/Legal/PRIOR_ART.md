# Novelty and Prior Art Statement

## What Is Novel

The individual component technologies in REHD are not new. Linear electromagnetic generators, capacitor banks, power management electronics, and directed energy weapons all exist independently. What is novel is their integration into a unified weapons-energy system where:

1. The recoil mechanism of a conventional gun serves simultaneously as a kinetic energy source for electrical generation
2. Generated power is dynamically routable to four distinct destinations via a unified power management system
3. The conventional gun and directed energy weapon are energetically coupled — firing one charges the other
4. A supplemental power bank accumulates combat-generated energy for asynchronous use across the ship's electrical grid
5. The fire control system has real-time authority to select and blend power modes based on tactical conditions

## Prior Art Search

To the inventor's knowledge, no existing system, proposed system, or published concept integrates recoil energy harvesting with directed energy weapon power supply as a unified naval weapons-energy architecture with dynamic mode selection.

Related but distinct prior art:
- Regenerative braking in electric vehicles (energy harvesting concept, different domain)
- Wave energy linear generators (linear generator technology, different application)
- Electro-thermal chemical gun research (electromagnetic gun enhancement, different energy source)
- Shipboard energy storage systems (energy storage, not coupled to gun recoil)
