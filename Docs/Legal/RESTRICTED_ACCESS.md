# Restricted Access — Mode 3 Content

## What Is Restricted

Mode 3 (Kinetic Energy Assist) of the REHD system describes a technology that may be subject to:

- **ITAR (International Traffic in Arms Regulations):** 22 CFR Parts 120-130, governing the export of defense articles and services
- **Invention Secrecy Act of 1951:** 35 U.S.C. §§ 181-188, authorizing the government to impose secrecy orders on patent applications detrimental to national security
- **EAR (Export Administration Regulations):** 15 CFR Parts 730-774, governing dual-use technologies

The inventor has proactively redacted Mode 3 implementation details from the public release as a precautionary measure. This is NOT a government-imposed restriction — it is a voluntary decision by the inventor to act responsibly.

## What Remains Public

The following is fully disclosed in the public release:

- The existence of Mode 3 as a concept within the REHD architecture
- The general principle that stored recoil energy can be used for electromagnetic projectile assist
- All other modes (1, 2, 4) with complete technical descriptions
- Full energy budget calculations
- System architecture for Modes 1, 2, and 4
- Claims 1–7

The restricted version exists in a private GitHub repository with a creation timestamp of March 13, 2026. The public disclosure of Mode 3's existence and general concept — combined with the private repository timestamp — establishes prior art for the full Mode 3 implementation as of this date.

## Note on Classification

The inventor is not a government employee and has no access to classified information. The REHD concept, including Mode 3, was developed entirely from publicly available information and original thinking. The voluntary redaction is a precautionary measure based on the inventor's understanding of ITAR and the Invention Secrecy Act, not a response to any government directive.

If the U.S. government determines that Mode 3 content does not require restriction, the inventor will promptly release it into the public version.
